[t00]USTracks v1.0 Beta [t01]USTracks are compatible with DBTracks and provide typical North American types of track ballast. Track selection will be expanded to Xtracks compatibility. Currently it is not yet complete.
All objects have beta status and are subject of further improvement. Use it on your own risk. Feedback and test reports are welcome. [t02]Meaning of the file names
US*_... - flat track bed, spacing between multiple tracks. All switches are of this type
US*b_... - flat track bed with hard shoulders, no spacing between multiple tracks
US*h_... - high ballast with hard shoulders, manual installation and manual lowering of terrain
US*he_... - high ballast with embankment, manual installation
US*b_, US*h_ and US*he_ are useful for main line track outside of yards and station areas only. [t03]Latest News [t010]History [t05] Xtracks Selection [t06] Size [t07] Pcs [t08] Created [t09] Remarks [t10] Inst.method [t102]US3  - ::
